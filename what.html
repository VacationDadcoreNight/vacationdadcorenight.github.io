Vacation Dadcore

What?

You may think that Vacation Dadcore is just an aesthetic, but it's so much more than that. It's a point of view, a mindset, a lifestyle.

Key Tenets

  • It's 5 o'clock somewhere 🍹
  • Relax... I'm on island time 🌴
  • No rain, no rainbows 🌈
  • Hang loose 🤙
  • Hakuna matata 🦁
  • No shirt, no shoes, no problem 🦵
  • You can't stop the waves... 🌊
    • but you can learn to surf 🏄‍♀️

Not a dad? Not on vacation? Not a problem! You don't need to be a father on PTO to embrace the core values of Vacation Dadcore. All you need is a drink in your hand and a vibe in your heart.

Whether you're throwing back a cold one, catching a gnarly swell, or just lounging poolside, anyone in the world can live the Vacation Dadcore life.
